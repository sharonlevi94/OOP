2.General Explaination:
In this exercise we draw a shapes to the screen: 
Rectangle, Isosceles triangle, Equilateral triangle and window.
The user input a coordinates for each shape everytime.
the shape apears on the screen of the console 4 times: 
a. The regular shape as the user inputed
b. The regular shape but with it's bounding rectangle.
c. The shape in larger or smaller scale. 
d. The shape in larger or smaller scale with it's right bounding rectangle.
